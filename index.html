Why Veggie Chicken?

Eco-Friendly

Vegan chicken production involves significantly lower greenhouse gas emissions, reduced land and water usage, and eliminates the ethical concerns associated with conventional chicken farming.

Economical

Instead of always opting to store-bought chicken from the store as a source of proteins, simply using flour is now a viable substiute for proteins, and is comparatively more cheap.

Ethical

As consumers become increasingly aware of the impact of their food choices on personal health and the environment, opting for vegan chicken emerges as a responsible and compassionate decision.

Method and Steps

  1. Step 1
    Prepare a bowl, fill it with flour and water according to the specified measurements.
  2. Step 2
    Mix well and knead until the texture of the dough is smooth.
  3. Step 3
    Rinse using boiled water 8 to 9 times.
  4. Step 4
    When the 8th or 9th rinse, the water has begun to clear, the water can be drained.
  5. Step 5
    Also make sure the texture of the dough looks more fibrous.
  6. Step 6
    Mix with galangal seasoning until evenly distributed to all sides of the dough while kneading slightly.
  7. Step 7
    Fry in nonstick Teflon and a little oil until browned.
